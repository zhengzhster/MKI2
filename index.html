MKI Database: a database of Macrocyclic Kinase Inhibitors

The MKI database, constructed by collecting all published MKIs with nanomolar activity from the scientific literature, paints a panorama of the current progress of MKIs, elucidates the characteristics of MKIs and scaffolds, and dissects the core structure of MKIs. It's free and will be updated timely that we hope it will facilitate the design and development of future MKIs.
